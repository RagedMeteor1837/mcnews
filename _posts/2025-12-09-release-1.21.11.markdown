Today Mounts of Mayhem charges into Minecraft, bringing a new weapon to wield, mounts to tame, foes to face, and strategies to master!<br>
So, riders of the Overworld, mount up and lead the charge in the Mounts of Mayhem drop!<br><br>

**CHANGELOG:**<br>
***New Features:***<br>
\* Added Nautilus and Zombie Nautilus<br>
\* Added Nautilus Armor<br>
\* Added Spear Weapon<br>
\* Added new advanceements<br>
\* Added new graphical options "Texture Filtering" and "Anisotropic Filtering"<br>
\* Added Netherite Horse Armor<br>

***Changes:***<br>
\* Zombies, Husks, Piglins and Zombified Piglins can now hold Spears<br>
\* Piglins now consider Golden Spears as one of the items they like<br>
\* Parrots can now mimic the sounds of Zombie Nautilus, Zombie Horse, Camel Husk and Parched<br>
\* Items will no longer "bob" whenever their durability changes, such as when losing durability from an attack, or repaired by Mending<br>
\* Some changes were made to debug renderers<br>
\* Graphics modes "Fast", "Fancy", and "Fabulous!" have been replaced with graphics presets with the same names<br>
\* Cloud movement is now the same for all players on a server<br>
\* Netherite Armor points have been increased for the Body slot from 11 to 19<br>
\* All blocks in the world now have mipmaps applied if enabled in video settings<br>
\* Client FPS is expected to be higher for most players<br>
\* Underwater biome fog color and fog distance is now blended based on the player's position between biomes, similarly to regular biome fog and sky colors<br>
\* Environmental Fog in the Nether is no longer dependent on the render distance setting, and will consistently be applied from 10 to 96 blocks<br>
\* Horses, Mules, Donkeys, and Camels no longer sink in water while ridden by a player<br>
\* Chunks now fade in instead of appearing out of nowhere, with the fade-in time adjustable through Video Settings<br>
\* Bats no longer spawn more frequently and in higher light levels between October 20 and November 3<br>
\* The color of the sky during sunrises and sunsets is now affected by rain and thunder<br>
\* Horses, Mules, Donkeys, Camels, Zombie Horses and Camel Husks will not panic if controlled by Mobs<br>
\* Spawn Eggs have been reorganized into groups in the Creative Inventory<br>
\* Mobs no longer spawn during chunk generation when the minecraft:spawn_mobs game rule is set to false.<br>
\* When the "See-Through Leaves" video setting is disabled, the gaps between leaves are now dark green instead of black<br>
\* Turning the "See-Through Leaves" video setting off now gives a bigger performance boost than before<br>
\* Added splash text "One does not simply walk to the Far Lands"<br>
\* Tamed horses no longer run into cacti or sweet berry bushes<br>
\* Removed Herobrine<br>

***Technical Changes:***<br>
\* The Data Pack version is now 94.1<br>
\* The Resource Pack version is now 75.0<br>
\* The Minecraft Server Management Protocol version is now 2.0.0<br>
\* Experience packets that do not change the player's experience progress or level are no longer sent by the server<br>
\* Added support for KQueue on OSX to improve network connection performance<br>

***Developer's Notes:***<br>
\* We've fixed 104 bugs in this release<br>
\* The new splash text "One does not simply walk to the Far Lands" was proven false on October 4, 2025<br>
\* View the [full changelog on Minecraft.net](https://www.minecraft.net/article/minecraft-java-edition-1-21-11)<br><br>
